About me

I began my journey into web development at the age of 18 through online tutorials on YouTube. Nowadays, I mainly work as a Frontend Web Developer, focusing on making the front part of websites looking and working great.

At first, I experimented with design, but I quickly recognized that I excelled more in coding. As a result, I dedicated myself to enhancing my coding skills, and you can explore some of my web projects in the Projects section.

Beyond coding, I have a deep passion for photography and share my work on Instagram, striving to showcase captivating content. I'm also actively looking for new job opportunities where I can use my skills and learn and grow. Let's connect and see what we can do together!
